Giftmart a Success!
Lunenburg Ledger - Letter to the Editor


"Lunenburg Family Giftmart – Holiday Gift Drive: The Lunenburg Giftmart Committee is planning their 6th annual Giftmart. Giftmart is being held in mid-December by invitation only through the Lunenburg Food Pantry and local churches. Donations of unwrapped gifts are displayed for folks to “shop” for their families, supplies are limited. Last year, Giftmart helped 80-plus Lunenburg families and the hope is to help more this year."
