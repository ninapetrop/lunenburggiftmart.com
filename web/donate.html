Donate

Give back to your community this holiday season by donating to Lunenburg Family Giftmart.

Suggested Donations

We are seeking donations of new toys and gifts for all ages to ensure that the members of our community have a wonderful holiday season. Families attending Giftmart are shopping for: babies, toddlers, teenagers, moms, dads, and grandparents.

  • Barbies/Dolls
  • Trucks/Cars
  • Toys
  • Crafts
  • Hoodies
  • Wallets/Purses
  • Sporting Goods
  • Stuffed Animals
  • Winter Coats/Hats/Mittens or Gloves
  • Puzzles/Games
  • Jewlery
  • Gift Cards/Certificates
  • Housewares
  • Legos
  • Holiday Gifts
  • Throw Blankets

Donation Drop Off Locations

Donations to Giftmart can be dropped off at the following locations:

  • Lunenburg Public Library
  • Lunenburg Primary School
  • The Boys and Girls Club
  • Public Safety Building
  • New Outlooks
  • Turkey Hill Middle School
  • Lunenburg High SChool
  • Cabel Salon
  • Lunenburg Senior Center

Monetary Donations

Monetary donations help provide a variety of gifts for our invited families to shop for. Monetary donations in the form of checks must be made payable to GIFTMART and can be mailed to:
