Donate

When you donate to Lunenburg Giftmart, you donate so much more than a gift.

We are seeking donations of new toys and gifts for all ages to ensure that the members of our community have a wonderful holiday season. Since our begining in 2009, the number of invited families to Giftmart has grown each year. Last year we invited over 100 families and thanks to the generous donations from families and businesses in Luenenburg, all of the 104 families were able to find the gifts they needed.

Help us make sure this years Giftmart is just as successful. See bellow for a list of suggested donations.
