About

The way Giftmart works is simple; we rely on donations from the community, either monetary or any new items which could be given as gifts. We are looking for toys, games, jewelry, warm clothing etc.

Invitations to shop at Giftmart are distributed the local food bank and through organizations who work with those families who may need support. Giftmart is set up for one day, during which our guests can “shop” for free in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. We provide gift wrapping, stocking stuffers and good wishes for a stress free holiday!
