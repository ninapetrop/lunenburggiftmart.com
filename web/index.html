Lunenburg Giftmart
Celebrating Community

Lunenburg Gitmart was created in 2009 because of an identified ned in our community. Thanks to the generous donations from families and businesses in Luenenburg, we proudly offer a way for families who may not have the resources for traditions such as gift giving an oppurtunity to "shop" for free.
