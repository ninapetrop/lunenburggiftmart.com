Lunenburg Giftmart in the Media

"Boy Scouts help sort donated toys" - Sentinel & Enterprise

"WORCESTER -- Several Cub Scouts from Lunenburg Pack 1728 worked alongside the 25th Marine Regiment at Toys for Tots on Dec. 8. The Cub Scouts traveled to 10 New Bond St. to help separate and bag toys for children.

They volunteered with the added benefit of knowing they were helping their local community when some of the toys separated and bagged went to Gift Mart in Lunenburg. Gift Mart has been helping families in need for many years now, and their one-day grand opening was Saturday."

"Giftmart a Success!" - Lunenburg Ledger Letter to the Editor

"The St. Boniface church hall was filled with hundreds of unwrapped gifts allowing over 100 families to shop for their holiday gift giving at no cost. Donations included toys for all ages, board games, crafts, warm clothing, sporting goods, and home decor, to name some of the items.

We are so fortunate to live in a community where folks come together to support one another." - Nancy Kerylow.

"It's heartwarming to experience the generosity of the community", states committe member Kathy Murray. The committee starts setting up 3 days prior to the event, organzing the donations for easy shopping and to ensure that all guests have the opportunity to take something home.
