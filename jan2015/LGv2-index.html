About

Our Mission

Lunenburg Giftmart was created in 2009 because of an identified need in our own community.

Through the generosity of our sponsors and community members we have been able to offer a way for families in our community to “shop” for free during the holiday season.

How we Work

Lunenburg Giftmart works in conjuction with the Lunenburg Lions club and a number of community sponsors that provide both monetary and physical gift donations. Each year invitations to Giftmart are distributed through the Lunenburg school system and Lunenburg Boys and Girls Club.

Collection boxes for donated gifts are located at various locations across Lunenburg throughout the year. For a complete list of donation locations please click here. Monetary donations are also used to purchase gifts for Giftmart invitees.

Our host, Saint Boniface, opens it’s doors to our invitees one day in December during a two part session where our invited families shop amongst the donated gifts. Need donation ideas? View our list of suggestions here.

Growing Every Year

Since 2009 the number of families invited to Giftmart has steadily grown. Last year we opened our doors to over 100 families, surpassing the prior year’s attendance of 80.

We look towards 2015 with eagerness to provide a merry holiday season to even more families and celebrate our beloved community.
