Lunenburg Giftmart is seeking donations of new toys and gifts for all ages to ensure that the members of our community in need have a wonderful holiday season. Donations will be distributed to families during a shopping day in mid December.

Monetary Donations

Monetary donations, of all amounts are also accepted. No donation amount is too small! Monetary donations are used to purchase gifts for age groups that we do not have enough of.
